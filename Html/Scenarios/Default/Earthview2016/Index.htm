Earth views 2016

A flyby over some of Earth's surface formations in SpaceXpanse 2016, featuring new textures and elevation maps.

Note that the default SpaceXpanse installation only comes with a low-resolution version of the Earth maps. To see the full supported resolution in SpaceXpanse as featured in the screenshots below, you need to install the full resolution maps. Go to the SpaceXpanse home page for links and installation instructions. Be sure to have plenty of free space on your hard drive!
