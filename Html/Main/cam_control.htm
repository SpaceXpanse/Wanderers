Camera control

The Control tab in the camera dialog allows to tilt and move the camera, or rotate it around the target, depending on the current camera mode.

The button functions of this tab replicate keyboard shortcuts Ctrl ←, Ctrl →, Ctrl ↑, Ctrl ↓, Ctrl PgUp, Ctrl PgDn, mouse camera control (right button pressed and mouse wheel), and and joystick coolie hat operation.
