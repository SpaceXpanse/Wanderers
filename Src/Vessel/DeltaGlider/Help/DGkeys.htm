Delta-glider keyboard controls

The following key functions are available for controlling Delta-glider vessels. These are in addition to the general SpaceXpanse keyboard controls.

BExtend airbrake by one step
Ctrl-BRetract airbrake by one step
DDeploy/retract radiators
GLower/raise gear
HCycle HUD modes
Ctrl-HDeploy/retract HUD screen
KOpen/close nosecone
OOpen/close outer airlock hatch
Ctrl-OOpen/close inner airlock hatch
UOpen/close top hatch
